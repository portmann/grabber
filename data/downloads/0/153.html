Dispute with suppliers interrupts output at six VW plants

Aug 22 2016, 03:36 ET | By: Yigal Grayeff, SA News Editor

Production at six Volkswagen (OTCPK:VLKAF) plants with over 25,000 workers has been hit as a dispute with two suppliers rumbles on.

The car-maker was today set to restart talks with CarTrim, which manufactures seats, and ES Automobilguss, which produces cast-iron parts for gearboxes.

The suppliers want compensation for what they say are lost revenues worth tens of millions of euros after VW canceled a contract without explanation.
