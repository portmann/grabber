Welcome to Wise Bread's Best Money Tips Roundup! Today we found articles on ways to get free shipping, travel tips you never thought of, and how to get a free honeymoon.

Top 5 Articles

5 Creative Ways to Get Free Shipping — When you're on the checkout page, open the chat window and simply ask for free shipping. Even if you don't get it, the retailer might give you coupons or offer other deals. [Money Talks News]

Expert Travel Tips You Never Thought Of — Bring a universal sink stopper with you so you can do laundry in the sink or use a bathtub that otherwise won't fill. [PopSugar Smart Living]

Free honeymoon? With rewards points, it's possible. — Wedding receptions are expensive, but since you'll be spending the money anyway, you can make it go farther by earning rewards from loyalty programs. The Monitor]

Dipping a Toe Into Minimalism: 7 Simple Ways to Try Minimalism Today! — Keep your bags packed and ready to go. Do this with purses, backpacks, and summer bags for outdoor activities. [Bargain Babe]

5 Easy College Dorm Decor Hacks for #BacktoSchool — Start with basic pieces like a plain lampshade which you can decorate with patterned paper. and tape. Simply change the paper when you're tired of the print. [Shoplet]

Other Essential Reading

6 Things to Consider Before Moving — If you aren't moving for a job, then the job outlook in the new area will be very important. Check sites like Monster, Career Builder, and even the jobs section of Craigslist to see what's available. [My Dollar Plan]

Frugal Rules for Savvier Shopping — Set internal price limits when you go shopping. If you decide you won't spend over $40 on a pair jeans, it'll be easier to set down a pair that costs $50. [Frugal Beautiful]

Take Advice From People Who Have What You Want! — Surround yourself with people who have accomplished what you want to do, learn from them, and take action. [Budgets Are Sexy]

How to Work Effectively With Others — We don't always get along with everyone, but it's important to find ways to work effectively together when we're in the workplace. [Dinks Finance]

How to Have a More Financially Responsible Mid-life Crisis — Follow these steps to handle a mid-life crisis without throwing your good finances out the window. [Money Mini Blog]
