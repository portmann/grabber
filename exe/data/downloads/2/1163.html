Most travelers know to go to popular travel sites to search quickly for the best rates available among a variety of airlines, hotels, and car rental companies. But there is one place that is often overlooked: Travel portals only available through your credit card. These travel portals offer cardholders exclusive discounts and offers that aren’t available anywhere else. (See also: Best Credit Cards for Online Booking Sites)

How Credit Card Travel Portals Work

Many credit card issuers have travel portals on their websites that allow you to book flights, hotels, and rental cars as well as cruises, tours, transfers, and other travel activities. Often the card issuer offers these travel portals as a place to spend rewards points or miles, and some even offer bonus rewards when you book travel this way. It’s something to keep in mind when you’re deciding on the best travel rewards credit card for you.

Bank of America Travel Center

The Bank of America Travel Center provides customers with access to over 200 airlines and 10,000 hotels and resorts. It also offers a Best Price Guarantee: if you purchase your travel through the Travel Center, then find it for less on another site, Bank of America will adjust your price or allow you to cancel with no penalty. 

BankAmericard Travel Rewards® Card

The BankAmericard Travel Rewards® card offers 1.5 points per dollar spent on most purchases, and points are worth one cent each as statement credits toward travel purchases that you make through any travel agency or travel provider. This card also offers 20,000 bonus points, worth $200 in travel statement credits, after using the card to make $1,000 in purchases within the first 90 days of account opening. Cardholders who have a Bank of America checking or savings account also earn a 10% annual customer relationship bonus on all of the points they earn each year. There is no annual fee for this card.

Click here to learn more and apply for the BankAmericard Travel Rewards® credit card today!

American Express Travel

When you use your eligible American Express card through American Express Travel, you can earn double Membership Rewards points on your purchases. You can also use the Pay With Points program to redeem your Membership Rewards points towards travel reservations. In addition, American Express Travel allows you to take advantage of complimentary benefits and competitive rates when booking with the Fine Hotels & Resorts or The Hotel Collection programs. Terms and conditions apply.

Amex EveryDay® Credit Card from American Express

The Amex EveryDay® Credit Card from American Express gives you an extra 20% points boost when you use your card 20 or more times in a billing period. Also, earn 2x points at US supermarkets (on up to $6,000 per year) and 1x points on other purchases. Get 10,000 bonus points after using your new card to make $1,000 in purchases within the first 3 months of account opening. There is no annual fee. Terms and conditions apply.

Click here to learn more and apply for the Amex EveryDay® Credit Card from American Express today!

Chase Ultimate Rewards Travel Portal

The Chase Ultimate Rewards Travel Portal allows cardholders to spend points earned in their rewards program. Certain cards offer a higher rewards redemption rate, like the Chase Sapphire Preferred which offers a 20% redemption discount when booking travel through the portal. Some cards also offer the added benefit of transferring points to airline and hotel loyalty programs, which can also be done through the portal.

Chase Sapphire Preferred® Card

The best card to use in Chase’s Travel Portal is the Chase Sapphire Preferred® Card, which offers the extra 20% redemption discount. The card offers 2X points on travel and dining at restaurants & 1 point per dollar spent on all other purchases worldwide. Earn 50,000 bonus points after you spend $4,000 on purchases in the first 3 months from account opening. That's $625 in travel when you redeem through Chase Ultimate Rewards®. If you don’t like what the Travel Portal has to offer, you can also transfer your points on a 1:1 rate to partner airlines and hotels.

Click here to learn more and apply for the Chase Sapphire Preferred® Card today!

Citi ThankYou Rewards Travel Center

The Citi ThankYou Rewards Travel Center is where you can spend ThankYou points earned through a Citi ThankYou credit card. Points earned from the ThankYou Preferred card are worth one cent each toward travel reservations made here but select Citi cards offer higher redemption value.

Citi Prestige® Card

With the Citi Prestige® Card from our partner Citi, points are worth 1.33 cents each toward most flight reservations, and 1.6 cents each towards flights booked on American Airlines. Cardholders earn 3 points for travel purchases, 2 points on dining and entertainment, and 1 point on other purchases. Earn 40,000 bonus points after spending $4,000 on purchases within the first 3 months of account opening. This premier card offers a large array of benefits, including a $250 annual air travel credit for fees, up to $100 in statement credit every 5 years toward Global Entry or TSA PreCheck application, AA airport lounge access, and more. There is a $450 annual fee.

Click here to learn more and apply for the Citi Prestige® Card today!
