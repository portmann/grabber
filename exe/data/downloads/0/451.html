Rebate site hot deals: Week of 8/22/2016

August 22, 2016 by John Wedding 2 Comments

Check out these rebate site hot deals for the week of August 22nd, 2016 …

Ebates – Triple Cash Back Week and 10% on Lenovo

This week (and this week only) is Ebates‘ Triple Cash Back Week!

Install the Ebates Cash Back Button to take advantage of the tripl-age.

There are also special deals if you download the Ebates app to your phone.

On top this week in addition to all of the fun events at Ebates is a 10% rebate from Lenovo!

Join Ebates for free and get a $10 Bonus credited after your first purchase!

Swagbucks – 12 SB per dollar at Jewelry.com

Swagbucks is fun. I probably say this too much but for 98% of the time it’s a real pleasure to use, and there are lots of extra contests and events to keep things very interesting.

Like there’s Swago this week (a portmanteau of Swagbucks and Bingo) where you can earn bonus SB for filling up your card with pretty designs.

Swagbucks this week is tied for top position at Jewelry.com with 12 SB/Dollar (equivalent to 12%)!

Sign up for free at Swagbucks to get in on the fun!

Active Junky – 12% Rebate at Under Armour

Active Junky is a rebate site that specializes in helping people save money with their purchases for the outdoors.

Now that the Olympics are done but maybe still fresh in our minds, suit up with a 12% rebate at Under Armour.  Active Junky is topping the list on this store this week by 4%!

Sign up for free at Active Junky to savor the savings at everyplace outdoors (and get a $5 bonus)!

Mr. Rebates – 12% Rebate at 1-800-FLOWERS

I’ve been a member of Mr. Rebates for quite a while and they’re very reliable with their rebate payouts.

Their Store of the Week this week is Origins, known for its high-performance skincare products, with a double rebate of 10%.

Guys:  When was the last time you surprised your lady with “just because” flowers? Mr. Rebates tops the list this week for 1-800-FLOWERS with a 12% rebate!

Join Mr. Rebates for free to get in on the action, and the savings!

Top Cash Back – More Student Treats Cashback Giveaway

Top Cash Back is often a good choice for a rebate site because of their consistently high rebates.  Currently they’re running a back-to-school fun time with tasks and prizes.  (I’ve won $0.75 solving easy-ish puzzles and catching a hummingbird; good times!  And I’m one box of crayons away from a $2 bonus!)

Each prize picked up and other things give you entries to a $1,500 giveaway!  That’s no small potatoes.  The giveaway runs through September 5th.

Sign up for free at Top Cash Back to catch some hummingbirds for some extra cash!

Rebate Codes – 5% cash back at Nature’s Products

Rebate Codes is a rebate site that was recently added to the cash-back comparison engine.  Nature’s Products came out on top of the heap recently with a 5% rebate!

Sign up for free at Rebate Codes to get some of this deal!

Be Frugal – 12% cash back at Wild Ties

At Be Frugal there is high cash back at Wild Ties – 12%.  There are some really crazy ones on this site.  We used to buy special ties with medical things on it for a doctor friend of ours.  They actually had an E. Coli tie.

Sign up for free at Be Frugal and get a $10 Bonus added to your account after you earn $25 in rebates!
