Don’t Take Them For Granted

Posted by jefferson in Health | 7 comments

There is somebody that you love that you haven’t seen in far too long.

Perhaps this person lives too far away from you, and it is just impractical for you to see each other.  But perhaps you have just both been busy, living your own lives. For some reason, neither of you have made it a priority to connect.

Please do me a favor.. and don’t take them for granted.

Give them a call, and do it today. Find a time when you can meet up for lunch or coffee. Invite them to come along the next time you hear about something interesting going on in your town. If they have plans and can’t make it, don’t get discouraged. Try again. Rinse and repeat.

We are all defined by the connections that we make in this world. The people that we love. and the relationships that we form, will play a big part in forming the identity of who we are.

People move, people get sick, and sometimes people die. Never wait until it’s too late to tend to the relationships that you already have.
